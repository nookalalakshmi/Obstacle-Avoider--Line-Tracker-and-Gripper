Re: Locked Atmega - 17th December 2002, 07:36 AM
I got mine to finally program. I tried all the fixes posted but none worked. I started playing around with the programmer and the card, then decided to connect the Atmega the opposite way and it worked. I believe that the jumpers on the Atmega were not making a good connection since there seemed to be enough play allowing the card to rock a little in its spot. Just thought you may want to try that before the other steps posted since it is the only thing that worked for me.
